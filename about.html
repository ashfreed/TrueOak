Our Team

John Billington

John has more than 30 years’ experience in the financial services sector, with the bulk of that time spent in wealth management. John has held many corporate roles including Managing Partner Finance Sector ANZ - Deloitte Consulting, Acting CFO - AXA Asia Pacific, General Manager Operations - IOOF and Executive Wealth – Bendigo/Adelaide Bank. John was also on the Board of Sandhurst Trustees. John has a risk/compliance background and is a qualified Organisation and Methods analyst.

Simone Thompson

Simone has close to 20 years’ experience in providing compliance, governance, and risk management services to financial institutions. Simone’s career in financial services began with a ten-year stint as a consultant with PFS Consulting, where she was heavily involved in projects for obtaining AFS Licences, RSE Licences, and MySuper Authorisations, for retail and industry super funds. Following this, Simone joined Australian Ethical Investment, where she was Head of Compliance in respect of the managed funds and the super fund, for two years. More recently, Simone has operated her own consultancy – ST Consult – where she has provided services to super fund promoters and boutique retail and wholesale fund managers, including Future Super, Verve Super, Professional Superannuation Management, and Manning Asset Management. Simone is also a Director of Togethr Trustees, the trustee of Equipsuper. At True Oak, Simone is responsible for coordinating the establishment of new CAR and Fund Manager arrangements, and for being the subject matter expert in financial services compliance.

Ash Freed

Ash has over 10 years experience in the financial services sector, predominantly within the superannuation industry. In her early career, Ash held a variety of roles across operations, technology and client services, lending to the development of a diverse set of skills and expertise across the industry. Ash’s experience during this time sparked her desire to pursue a career in software development and continues to inform the holistic, impact focused lens she brings to her role as a systems developer today. At True Oak, Ash is responsible for the development and maintenance of Folio, the contract and compliance software system True Oak uses to manage its clients.
